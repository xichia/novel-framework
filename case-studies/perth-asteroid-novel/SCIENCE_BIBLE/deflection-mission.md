# SCIENCE_BIBLE/deflection-mission.md

## Purpose in the Novel

The deflection mission serves three functions:

1. **Narrative closure** — it ends the countdown and begins the utopia
2. **Evidentiary erasure** — it makes post-hoc verification of the original threat impossible
3. **Mythological foundation** — it gives the utopia its sacred origin story

*We were doomed. We cooperated. We saved ourselves.*

This myth is the utopia's emotional infrastructure. It is what people teach their children. It is what the anniversary celebrates. It is what Martin will be asked to stand up and honor.

---

## Real-World Foundation

NASA's DART (Double Asteroid Redirection Test) mission, launched in November 2021 and impacting Dimorphos in September 2022, proved kinetic impactor deflection works. It changed Dimorphos's orbital period by 33 minutes — far more than predicted.

ESA's Hera mission (launched 2024) followed up to characterize the impact results.

This gives the novel's fictional deflection a real technical foundation. The conspiracy does not need to invent deflection technology. It needs only to apply real, proven technology to a manufactured crisis.

---

## The Fictional Mission

The novel's deflection mission is a near-future extrapolation of DART/Hera.

Key features:

**International cooperation** — the mission is explicitly multinational. This is both politically necessary (the conspiracy needs broad government buy-in) and symbolically essential (the cooperation is itself the point). NASA, ESA, JAXA, ISRO, Roscosmos, CNSA — all contribute. The mission becomes the first genuine joint human project of its kind.

**The kinetic impactor** — a spacecraft rams the asteroid at high speed, changing its orbit. The technology works. The object's orbit is genuinely altered. The deflection is real even though the threat was not.

**The announcement of success** — after a tense monitoring period, orbital refinement confirms the deflection worked. The impact probability drops to zero. The world exhales.

**The celebration** — globally coordinated. Civic events in every city. Perth has its own — a gathering on the foreshore, probably, or in a park. This event, or its anniversary, is what Martin may be asked to speak at.

## Why the Deflection Erases the Evidence

After the deflection, the asteroid's orbit has genuinely changed.

The pre-deflection orbital data exists in the MPC archives. But:

- The *processed* orbital solutions (the public record) were shaped by the conspiracy's nudged data
- The *raw* astrometric measurements (the original data) are archived but not routinely examined
- Comparing the two requires specialist knowledge, deliberate suspicion, and the specific tools Giacob spent a career developing

Post-deflection, no one has reason to look. The object is safe. The mission succeeded. The crisis is over.

The conspiracy's physical evidence is not destroyed. It is made irrelevant by success.

## The Myth's Structure

The deflection mission produces a founding myth with a specific emotional architecture:

**The threat** — humanity faced extinction. Not metaphorical extinction. Actual, physical, civilizational.

**The choice** — faced with this, humanity chose cooperation over conflict. Wars paused. Borders opened. Resources flowed where they were needed.

**The mission** — the cooperation produced something real: a coordinated planetary defense mission. The cooperation was not just moral. It was practical. It saved lives.

**The deliverance** — the deflection worked. Humanity survived because it chose, at the last moment, to act as one.

This myth is genuinely moving. It is also entirely false. And Martin is the only ordinary person who knows it.

## The Anniversary Event

The pressure event is the anniversary of the deflection confirmation — the day the world learned it had survived.

In the present narrative (Strand A), this anniversary is approaching. The world prepares to celebrate. Perth plans a civic gathering — the kind of community event that is now routine in the utopia. A shared meal, perhaps. A gathering at the foreshore. Children. Speeches. The retelling of the myth.

Martin may be asked to speak. He has become, in his neighborhood, in his community, a person people listen to. His history — the years of fighting for working people, the collapse, the retreat, the transformation — makes him a credible voice for what the world was before and what it became after.

He is asked to stand up in front of people he loves and celebrate a lie.

This is the novel's central pressure. Not a ticking clock. A civic invitation.

## The Mission's Emotional Resonance in Perth

Perth's relationship to the deflection mission is specific.

Perth has one of the most significant optical astronomy facilities in the Southern Hemisphere. The observatory history of Western Australia — Mount Stromlo's involvement in tracking satellites during the Space Age, Parkes, the deep space tracking stations at New Norcia — means Perth has a genuine relationship with space that is not merely metropolitan.

In the novel, it would be natural for Perth to have been one of the observatories involved in tracking the asteroid. This gives Martin's community a specific, local connection to the event they are celebrating.

It also means that Giacob, in coming to Perth, has chosen a city with its own thread in the conspiracy's technical history. Whether he knew this consciously is a question worth holding.
