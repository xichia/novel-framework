### MPC Data Submission and Verification

**Question:** How does the Minor Planet Center receive, verify, and publish astrometric data? Is raw data publicly archived?

**Why it matters:** The conspiracy's mechanism depends on nudging astrometric data at the submission level. Need to know how detectable this would be.

**Current knowledge:** The MPC is at the Center for Astrophysics, Harvard. Observatories submit observations in a standard format (MPC format). The MPC publishes orbital solutions derived from submitted observations. Raw observations are published in the MPC database — publicly accessible. Processed orbital solutions come from these raw observations.

**Implication:** The raw nudged data would be in the public record. Giacob's evidence package to Martin would include comparison of the submitted raw data against what the physical reality should have produced — a technically demanding but not impossible forensic reconstruction.

### DART Mission Technical Details

**Question:** What exactly did DART do, and what would a fictional next-generation deflection look like?

**Current knowledge:** DART impacted Dimorphos (moonlet of Didymos) on 26 September 2022 at approximately 6.1 km/s. Changed Dimorphos's orbital period around Didymos by 33 minutes. The mission demonstrated that kinetic impactor deflection is viable with years of warning time.

**Fictional extrapolation:** A larger target, a more powerful or multiple-impactor approach, international cooperation on the mission design. DART was a test; the fictional mission is the real thing.

---

### The Warning Timeline

**Question:** How much warning time is realistic for a meaningful kinetic deflection mission?

**Current knowledge:** For a kinetic impactor to work, you need ideally 10+ years of warning — enough time to design, build, launch, and execute the mission. With less than 5 years, options become severely limited. Less than 2 years, a kinetic impactor is essentially impossible.

**Implication for the novel:** The conspiracy must run for years before the public announcement. The timeline in the science bible (Year minus 5 to public announcement) is realistic. The object's discovery and the conspiracy's intervention both happen years before the world is told.

## Priority 3 — For Accuracy and Texture

### Perth's Astronomical Connection

**Question:** What specific observatories or tracking facilities exist in or near Perth?

**Current knowledge:** The New Norcia deep space tracking station, operated by ESA, is approximately 130 km north of Perth. The Perth Observatory (Bickley) has historical significance. The Zadko Telescope at Gingin is specifically used for asteroid follow-up observations.

**Implication:** The Zadko Telescope is potentially significant. It is a real facility, operated by the University of Western Australia, with a specific mandate for near-Earth object follow-up. It is plausible that a Perth-adjacent telescope contributed to observations of the conspiracy's object. This would give Perth a specific, technical connection to the event.

Whether the Zadko data was among the nudged data is a question worth holding.
