# Seasons and Class

## Status

Scaffold.

## Seasonal Architecture

Before countdown: Perth winter. Grey, wet, cold, solitary.

Countdown: late winter into spring. Human warmth begins to break through.

Present utopia: late summer into autumn. Golden, settled, cooling, communal.

The movement toward winter becomes hygge rather than despair.

## Class Architecture

Martin is working-class, gifted, and shaped by the knowledge that talent does not guarantee dignity in an unfair world.

The utopia gives him something class never did:

He does not have to fight to be seen.
