This document reviews `case-studies/perth-asteroid-novel/BRANCHES/chapter-17-packet-v1.md` to evaluate whether it is ready to guide prose drafting for Chapter 17.

---

## 2. Executive Verdict

**Ready with Minor Revisions**

The Chapter 17 packet is structurally robust and represents a highly effective blueprint for drafting. It successfully captures the core narrative burden of the novel—the moment the global asteroid hoax is transferred to Martin as a "cold secret inside a warm room"—and establishes strong guards against genre collapse, melodrama, and exposition dumps. 

However, the packet currently over-prescribes the staging details of the prose probe (treating the wine glass, the standing posture, the Mundijong oranges, and the final "No" as rigid requirements rather than optional discoveries). It also lacks guidance on transitioning the scene from the warm residue of the friendship built in Chapters 15–16, and contains a slight tension regarding Martin's agency (active lawyering vs. detective work). Once these items are revised to preserve flexibility for prose discovery, the packet will be fully ready to guide drafting.

---

## 3. Strengths

*   **Exposition Discipline:** The "Technical Exposition Limits" section (restricting Giacob to maximum three-sentence bursts and mandating environmental interruptions) is exceptionally strong. It ensures the mathematical and orbital details do not stall the dramatic momentum.
*   **Giacob's Restraint:** The handling notes for Giacob are excellent, framing him as clinically exhausted, physically declining, and completely stripped of vanity or a desire for forgiveness. This protects the scene from deathbed melodrama.
*   **Anti-Thriller Protections:** The packet successfully avoids thriller tropes by ensuring the evidence package lacks a ticking clock, denying Martin physical danger, and keeping the conspiracy an institutional bureaucracy rather than a cast of targetable villains.
*   **Domestic Grounding:** The packet correctly emphasizes the juxtaposition of the civilizational hoax against mundane, domestic objects, maintaining the literary register of the novel.

---

## 4. Risks

*   **Over-prescription of Staging Details:** By hardcoding the specific props (drying a neighbor's wine glass, Mundijong oranges) and physical postures (both men standing, ending on the word "No") into the Beat Map and Drafting Checklist, the packet risks turning a successful prose probe into a rigid template. This limits the writer's capacity for organic prose discovery.
*   **Abrupt Friendship Freeze:** The packet lists the pre-existing friendship from Chapters 15–16 as a required input, but does not provide guidance on how to dramatize the immediate, warm residue of that friendship before the tone drops. Without this, the transition into the confession risks feeling sterile and unearned.
*   **Detective Creep:** There is a minor contradiction between the warning to "Ensure Active Lawyering" and the constraint to "prevent Martin from becoming a detective." If not carefully defined, the drafting model may have Martin cross-examine Giacob in a way that feels like an active investigation of a crime, rather than a shocked defense mechanism.
*   **Thriller MacGuffin Risk via "The Names":** The evidence package details include "the names" of the conspirators. Highlighting this in dialogue risks shifting Martin's focus toward exposing individuals, turning the files into a classic thriller MacGuffin.

---

## 5. Required Changes Before Drafting

1.  **Relax Staging Details to Optional Discoveries:** 
    Modify the Beat Map and Drafting Checklist to frame the wine glass, the standing posture, the Mundijong oranges, and the final "No" as *exemplary prose discoveries* rather than rigid mandates. The checklist should verify that the scene achieves the underlying *functions* (domestic grounding, physical suspension, domestic intrusion, and unresolved silence) rather than demanding these specific props and words.
2.  **Dramatize the Warm Friendship Residue:**
    Add an instruction in the Beat Map and Handling Notes to open the scene with a brief, natural interaction that carries the quiet warmth and routine of their established friendship (e.g., sharing tea, discussing the garden, or a comfortable silence). This ensures the reader feels the pain of the friendship freezing when the confession begins.
3.  **Clarify the Boundary of Active Lawyering:**
    Specify that Martin's "active lawyering" is a *psychological defense mechanism* to process shock, not an investigation. His clarifying questions must target the logistics and credibility of the data to verify what he is hearing, rather than investigating the identities or motives of the wider conspiracy.
4.  **Downplay "The Names" in Dialogue:**
    Instruct the writer that while the evidence package contains institutional names, they must not become a subject of active dialogue or curiosity in this scene. The emphasis must remain on the statistical and institutional data that proves the hoax.

## 6. Optional Improvements

*   **Dynamic Seasonal Light:** Explicitly suggest utilizing the Perth autumn light as a visual transition—starting with warm afternoon amber reflecting off domestic surfaces and fading to cool, flat twilight as the confession ends.
*   **Sensory Shock Cues:** Provide optional cues for Martin's physical registration of shock (e.g., the sudden dryness of his throat, the sound of the refrigerator hum rising to fill the room, or the weight of the damp cloth in his hand) to balance the clinical dialogue.
