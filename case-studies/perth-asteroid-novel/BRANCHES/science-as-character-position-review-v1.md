## 2. Executive Verdict

**Accept**

The "Science as Character" position note is a vital and timely methodological correction. It successfully resolves a passive drift in the project's planning where the warning to "avoid exposition" was being misinterpreted as a mandate to "minimize science." By reframing science as an active, dramatic force, the note restores the intellectual and atmospheric texture of the novel without compromising its literary standards. The proposed guidelines align perfectly with the needs of Chapter 17, providing a clear path for delivering the asteroid hoax data in a way that maximizes narrative tension and reader engagement.

---

## 3. Strengths

*   **Reframing of Exposition:** The note correctly and cleanly distinguishes "inert exposition" (lecturing the reader on physics) from "dramatic science" (inviting the reader to think alongside the characters). This preserves pacing while welcoming technical depth.
*   **Martin's Translation Lens:** Connecting the scientific data to Martin's background as an employment lawyer is a brilliant narrative solution. Framing the orbital math and coordinate discrepancies as a "death sentence with paperwork around it" grounds the physics in his character voice and institutional expertise.
*   **Maintenance of Restraint:** The note successfully balances the engaging, problem-solving energy of *Project Hail Mary* and accessible wonder with the novel's required gravity. It explicitly demands that science remain anchored to scene pressure and character interiority.
*   **Intellectual Engagement:** The "Think Along" rule creates reader pleasure, ensuring the mechanics of the hoax feel mathematically and institutionally plausible rather than like generic thriller magic.

---

## 4. Risks

*   **The Tone Match: Neil deGrasse Tyson vs. Literary Melancholy:** While the Tyson reference is useful for "accessible wonder," there is a risk that the drafting model might adopt a voice that is too conversational, enthusiastic, or optimistic. The wonder in this novel must remain cold, vast, and tinged with the melancholy of a faked apocalypse.
*   **Didactic Slippage:** In attempting to make the science accurate and interesting, the prose could drift into pop-science writing. The author must ensure that every scientific detail is filtered through a character's emotional burden or physical state.
*   **Over-complicating the Hoax:** Introducing too many variables (e.g., specific sensor names, telescope models, telemetry protocols) could clutter the dialogue, overriding the three-sentence rule.

---

## 5. Required Changes Before Adoption

No structural revisions to the position note are required. However, the following constraint must be enforced during drafting:
*   *Tonal Filter:* All scientific wonder and explanation must be strictly filtered through Martin's melancholy and Giacob's physical exhaustion. The "awe" must feel like planetary fragility and cold cosmic indifference, never like an enthusiastic lecture.

## 7. Chapter 17 Drafting Implications

The position note provides immediate, practical guidance for Chapter 17. The current version of the chapter packet (`chapter-17-packet-v2.md`) does **not** need a new revision or addendum. The position note can simply be passed as a primary style input to the Chapter 17 drafting prompt alongside the packet.

### Integration with Acceptance Criteria:
*   **Prose-Probe Staging:** Treating the science as a dramatic force supports relaxing the staging details (glass, standing). The focus shifts to how the physical space reacts to the pressure of the numbers, letting the writer discover natural props.
*   **Friendship Residue:** The shared appreciation for science and order provides a natural baseline for the opening beats of warm interaction before the cold confession starts.
*   **Martin's Lawyer Brain:** The note directly reinforces this constraint by defining his active lawyering as a psychological defense (translating statistics to make shock manageable) rather than detective work.
*   **Conspirator Names:** The note keeps the focus on data logs and astrometry, keeping the evidence package from becoming a thriller MacGuffin.
*   **Anti-Exposition & Giacob's Frailty:** The note preserves the three-sentence limit by demanding that science be interrupted by Giacob's physical decline, contrasting cosmic scale with his mortal body.
*   **Canon Hygiene:** The note introduces no new facts, keeping orbital numbers branch-only.
