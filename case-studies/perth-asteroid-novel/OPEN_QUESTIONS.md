### OQ-002 — The Elder: Name

**Status:** Active — ready to decide

**Question:** What is the elder's name?

**Options discussed:** Laszlo, Edmund, Heinrich, François

**Notes:** Should feel European and institutional — the name of a man who has sat in serious rooms for a long time. Not glamorous. Not eccentric. Quietly authoritative. Eastern European names (Laszlo) carry a particular Cold War institutional resonance. Western European names (François, Heinrich) feel more aligned with Geneva/UN culture.

**Downstream impact:** Medium. Affects character files and all references throughout drafting.

### OQ-003 — Martin's Specific Career Collapse

**Status:** Active

**Question:** What exactly was the case that broke Martin's legal career?

**Options:**
- A major enterprise bargaining case where union leadership accepted a deal that protected the institution and abandoned the workers. Martin was asked to sell it. He refused or complied and couldn't live with it.
- A wrongful dismissal case involving a vulnerable worker that was settled against Martin's advice for institutional reasons.
- A systemic discrimination case that was wound back by a sympathetic union leadership afraid of political exposure.

**Notes:** The case needs to be specific enough to feel real. It should have a named (or nameable) industry — mining, healthcare, retail, aged care. The betrayal should come from inside Martin's own side, not from the employer. That is what makes it definitive.

**Downstream impact:** High. This scene will likely appear in Strand B and defines Martin's final disillusionment.

### OQ-004 — Martin's Post-Collapse Role

**Status:** Active

**Question:** What does Martin do after losing his legal career?

**Options:** Library assistant, council worker, civic admin role, something in a community legal centre in a reduced capacity.

**Notes:** Must be beneath his abilities but not humiliating. Still connected to public life. The role should feel like a man who has not abandoned his values but has stopped believing they can change anything.

**Downstream impact:** Medium. Affects Strand B and the utopia-present where his role may have transformed.

### OQ-005 — The Elder's Nationality

**Status:** Active

**Question:** Is the elder specifically French, German, Hungarian, Swiss, or another European nationality?

**Notes:** His nationality will shape his personal history, the specific institutional rooms he inhabited, and the texture of the European strand. A Czech or Hungarian physicist would carry Cold War and post-communist resonance. A French scientist would fit Geneva culture naturally. A German scientist would carry its own institutional weight.

### OQ-006 — The Meeting Scene

**Status:** Active

**Question:** Where and how exactly do Martin and the elder meet?

**Notes:** Must be unforced and specific to Perth. Candidates:
- A café near the river
- The State Library of Western Australia
- A community event in the utopia — a shared meal, a civic gathering
- Walking along the foreshore

The elder should be visibly out of place in a way Martin notices but doesn't find threatening. Their first conversation should feel like neither of them planned it.

**Downstream impact:** High. This is a pivotal scene likely in the opening chapters of Strand A.

### OQ-007 — Structure of the Evidence Package

**Status:** Active

**Question:** What exactly does the elder leave Martin?

**Confirmed:** A recorded confession, written account, technical documents, institutional correspondence, names, and enough material to verify the fabrication.

**Open:** What form does this take physically? A drive, a sealed envelope, a document case, something else? And how does Martin receive it — before the elder's death, at the moment of death, or after?

### OQ-008 — The Pressure Event

**Status:** Active

**Question:** What is the specific external circumstance that makes Martin's silence increasingly active?

**Confirmed:** An approaching anniversary of the deflection. The world is celebrating the event that supposedly saved humanity.

**Open:** What form does the pressure take for Martin specifically? He may be invited to speak at a civic ceremony, a school event, a community library gathering. This should feel entirely plausible for a man in his position in the utopia.

**Downstream impact:** High. This is the novel's external pressure engine.

### OQ-010 — Secondary Characters

**Status:** Active — needs development

**Question:** Who are the key secondary characters in the present utopia?

**Notes:** Martin needs people around him — neighbors, friends, perhaps a colleague. These characters embody the utopia's texture. At least one should be someone who would be devastated by the truth. This creates additional moral weight for Martin's decision.

**Downstream impact:** High. Secondary characters carry the utopia's daily texture and deepen Martin's dilemma.
