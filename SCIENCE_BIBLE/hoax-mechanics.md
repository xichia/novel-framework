# SCIENCE_BIBLE/hoax-mechanics.md

## Overview

The hoax works because it exploits institutional trust rather than fabricating physical reality.

The asteroid is real. The uncertainty is real. The cooperation of a small number of key institutional actors is what converts a manageable uncertainty into a global emergency.

---

## The Object: Candidate Profile

The conspiracy selects an asteroid with the following characteristics:

**Genuine discovery:** Detected by a legitimate survey telescope. Logged in the MPC database. Observable by independent astronomers worldwide.

**Genuine initial uncertainty:** Short observational arc producing a real, non-trivial impact probability. Something that, if the orbit refinement process were left alone, would resolve to safe over six to eighteen months. The Sentry and Meerkat systems would flag it briefly before clearing it.

**Media attention:** The object has already attracted some public interest — a brief news cycle, a NASA statement about "continued monitoring," the usual language of managed uncertainty.

**Suitable size:** Large enough to be genuinely extinction-level threatening — enough to justify global response. Small enough that its orbital uncertainty is plausible given real asteroid science.

In the novel's world, this object might be analogous to real near-Earth asteroids that generated brief concern — Apophis (2004), Bennu, 2023 DW — before orbital refinement cleared them.

## The Mechanism in Four Acts

### Act One — Selection and Preparation (Years Before Announcement)

The conspiracy identifies the object during its genuine uncertainty window.

Key conspirators inside three to five major observatories begin submitting astrometric data with subtle biases — measurements shifted by fractions of arcseconds, enough to maintain apparent impact probability when natural refinement would cause it to drop.

Giacob's role: he advises on the specific technical parameters. He knows the exact magnitude of bias required to maintain a probability above the classified briefing threshold without triggering obvious inconsistency with the physical reality.

The biased data is indistinguishable from measurement error — which is always present in astrometry. This is the conspiracy's technical genius: it exploits the normal noise floor of astronomical measurement.

### Act Two — Institutional Escalation (Months Before Public Announcement)

The impact probability, sustained by nudged data, crosses the threshold for classified government notification.

Giacob coordinates the language of the risk communication — the specific framing that will make governments respond rather than wait for further refinement.

He knows from long experience that governments respond not to probabilities but to institutional consensus. He ensures that consensus forms before the independent scientific community can contradict it.

IAWN and SMPAG are briefed. A small number of key figures in these bodies are either conspirators or have been professionally positioned to accept the consensus framing.

### Act Three — Public Announcement

The world is told.

The announcement feels like a discovery, not a declaration, because it *is* a discovery — of a real object, by real institutions, following real protocols. The conspiracy has not fabricated the announcement. It has shaped the institutional machinery that produces it.

From this point, the conspiracy no longer needs to actively maintain the hoax. The object is real. Independent observers can find it. Its orbital uncertainty, now locked into the public record, supports the official probability. The institutional momentum is self-sustaining.

What the conspiracy has done cannot be undone by independent observation — because the independent observation confirms a real object with a genuinely uncertain orbit.

### Act Four — The Deflection and Erasure

A deflection mission is authorized by international consensus.

The mission is real. A spacecraft is launched. It reaches the object. A kinetic impactor changes the object's orbit.

The world celebrates.

The original orbital data — the pre-deflection record that would show the conspiracy's nudges — exists in the MPC archives. But interpreting it now requires:
1. The specific technical expertise Giacob has
2. Access to the original raw data rather than the processed records
3. The prior knowledge that something is wrong to look for

Post-deflection, no one is looking. The object's orbit has changed. The threat is gone. The celebration begins.

The conspiracy's evidence does not disappear. It is buried under success.

## What Giacob Leaves Martin

The evidence package must be sufficient for verification but require specialist knowledge to use.

Giacob leaves:

**The recorded confession** — his account of the conspiracy, his role, the names of key participants, the mechanism in plain language.

**The original astrometric data** — the raw measurement files from the three to five compromised observatories, alongside the published records. The discrepancies are small but systematic and statistically impossible as random error.

**The institutional correspondence** — emails, meeting notes, classified briefing documents. The conspiracy's paper trail, accumulated over years.

**The orbital analysis** — Giacob's own reconstruction of what the unbiased orbit would have shown: a near miss, not an impact. The mathematical proof that the conspiracy's nudges were what maintained the probability.

**Names** — the key conspirators still living, their institutional affiliations, their current locations.

Martin is not a scientist. He cannot verify the orbital analysis himself. But the evidence is structured so that any qualified independent astrophysicist could verify it in weeks.

Martin's legal training is relevant here. He knows how to assess a documentation package. He knows what constitutes proof. He knows the difference between allegation and evidence.

He also knows, from his legal career, that truth and justice are not the same thing.

## The Timeline

The conspiracy unfolds over years, not months.

**Year minus 5 to minus 3:** Object selected. Compromised observatories begin systematic data nudging. Giacob brought in.

**Year minus 2:** Impact probability sustained above classified briefing threshold. Government notifications begin.

**Year minus 1:** Public announcement. Global response begins.

**Year zero:** Deflection mission launches.

**Year plus 1:** Deflection confirmed successful. Global celebration. The sacred origin myth crystallizes.

**Present (Strand A):** Several years after the deflection. The utopia is established. Giacob is dying. Martin is happy.

The approaching anniversary — the pressure event — is the five or ten year anniversary of the deflection. The world is preparing to celebrate the day humanity saved itself.

## The Number Who Know

Active conspirators who understand the full picture: approximately 20–30 people worldwide.

A larger number participated in specific acts — the compromised observatory data, the institutional coordination — without understanding the full scope. They believed they were part of a legitimate if classified crisis response. They are not conspirators; they are instruments.

Of the 20–30 who know everything, most are aging. Several have died. Giacob is one of the last who still carries the full picture.

This is another reason he goes to Perth. He has watched the others die with it. He cannot.
